Who We Are

NayePankh Foundation is dedicated to transforming lives through grassroots educational initiatives, skill development courses, and social empowerment projects.

Our Mission

Empowering youth through education and social impact initiatives. We aim to break the cycle of poverty by providing primary academic assistance, career counseling, and technological literacy to students who need it the most.

Through our dedicated volunteer networks, we build resources that enable rural communities to develop sustainable solutions for local challenges.

Our Vision

Creating a society where every individual has equal opportunities to learn, grow, and thrive. We visualize a future where zip codes or socioeconomic backgrounds do not dictate a child's academic or career success.

We work to foster communities where mutual support, empathy, and collective growth form the foundation of day-to-day interactions.
